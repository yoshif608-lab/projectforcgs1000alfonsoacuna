Academics at HC

Career interest / major focus: Data Analysis

At Hillsborough College, I am completing an Associate of Arts (A.A.) with a Cybersecurity specialization. HC's A.A. is a 60-credit transfer degree — 43 hours of core curriculum plus 17 hours of transferable electives focused on my intended major, giving me a strong technical foundation in security and systems before I move into data-focused coursework at a university.

HC's Cybersecurity program is recognized because its courses align with industry certifications such as CompTIA Security+ and Network+.
