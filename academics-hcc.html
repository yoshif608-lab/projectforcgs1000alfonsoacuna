Academics at HCC

Career interest / major focus: Data Analysis

At Houston Community College, I am completing an Associate of Arts (A.A.) with a Cybersecurity specialization. HCC's A.A. is a 60-credit transfer degree — 43 hours of core curriculum plus 17 hours of transferable electives focused on my intended major, giving me a strong technical foundation in security and systems before I move into data-focused coursework at a university.

HCC's Cybersecurity program is recognized by the National Security Agency as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense, and its courses align with industry certifications such as CompTIA Security+ and Network+.

HCC Courses in My Program

  • English Composition I & II
  • College Algebra / Introductory Statistics
  • Networking Fundamentals
  • Security Fundamentals
  • Operating Systems (Linux & Windows)
  • Programming & Scripting Foundations
  • Computer Virtualization
  • Network Analysis & Firewalls
