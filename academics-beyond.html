Academics Beyond HCC

After finishing my A.A. at HCC, I plan to transfer into a data-focused bachelor's program, most likely at the University of South Florida (USF) or the University of Central Florida (UCF). Here are three programs I'm considering:

USF · Tampa

B.S. Business Analytics & Information Systems

Offered through USF's Muma College of Business, this program blends business fundamentals with data analytics and systems development, preparing students to turn data into decisions for organizations of any size.

Why it fits: It builds directly on the technical base I'm getting at HCC while adding the business context data analysts need.

UCF · Orlando

B.S. in Data Science

An interdisciplinary degree run jointly by UCF's Computer Science, Statistics and Data Science, Mathematics, and Industrial Engineering departments, focused on algorithm design, data mining, and interpreting large datasets.

Why it fits: Heavier on statistics and programming — great if I want to go deeper into the technical side of analytics.

USF · Tampa

B.S. Information Science, Data Science & Analytics Concentration

A concentration within USF's Information Science degree that focuses specifically on data-driven decision-making and analytics tools.

Why it fits: A middle path between the business and the pure statistics routes, with flexibility to pick electives that match where I end up wanting to specialize.
